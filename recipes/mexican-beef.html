Mexican Beef

Description

Juicy, succulent slow cooked Mexican spiced beef. Goes great in a variety of dishes including tacos, burritos and enchaladas.

Ingredients

Spice Mix

  • 1/2 tbsp chipotle powder
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp All Spice powder (ground All Spice)
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder or garlic powder OR 1 tsp of each
  • 1 tsp salt and pepper

Beef

  • 1 - 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 lb / 1.5kg beef chuck or brisket (or gravy or any other slow cooking beef) cut into 4 pieces
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 onion , diced (yellow, brown or white)
  • 3/4 cup (185 ml) orange juice
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 14 oz / 400g can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups (500 ml) beef or chicken broth/stock
  • 1/2 cup (125ml) water
  • Salt and pepper
